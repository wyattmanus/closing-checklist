⏰ 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Clean and shut down blender (wipe exterior, rinse pitcher, store properly)
Clean group heads (backflush, wipe gaskets and screens)
Wipe down syrup rack (remove drips, wipe bottles and shelf)
Wipe down all counters and surfaces
Pre-count cash drawer and record totals

⏰ 8:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Shut down back machine (if slow enough)
Remove syrup toppers and clean lids
Restock machines (cups, lids, sleeves, straws)
Fill sauces and syrups to appropriate levels
Close shot drawer
Clean and sanitize steam wands
Clean all rapid rinsers except front machine
Stock fridge (milks, creams, cold brew, etc.)
Stock machine (syrups, sauces, dry goods)
Close out Lane 2 drawer (if slow enough)

⏰ 9:00 PM – 10:00 PM

Shut down third group head
Deep clean steam wands and portafilters
Wipe down all equipment exteriors
Sweep and mop floors
Take out trash and replace liners
Wipe down drive-thru window and ledge

🔒 10:00 PM — Final Close

Flush and close remaining group head(s)
Clean chamber with grinds brush
Lock wheelbarrow
Condense, Pay in, Pay out tips (SL ONLY)
Final wipe down of all surfaces and equipment
Shut down all remaining machines and equipment
Count and close final cash drawer

🔐 Lock-Up

Lock walk-in cooler/freezer
Lock wheelbarrow door
Lock lane doors
Lock stand
Lock walkup doors
Lock snowblower door

✅ Shift Lead Sign-Off
